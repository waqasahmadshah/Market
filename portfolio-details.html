Wavely App Rebrand

A complete brand and UX transformation for a fintech startup that increased signups by 120% and earned industry recognition.

Client

Wavely Financial, Inc.

Services

Branding, UX/UI Design, Development

Year

2023

Project Overview

Wavely approached us with a challenge: transform their fintech app from a functional tool into a beloved brand that resonates with millennials. Despite having a solid product, they were struggling to stand out in a crowded market and connect emotionally with their target audience.

Our task was to create a comprehensive rebrand that would not only refresh their visual identity but also improve the user experience to drive engagement and conversion. The goal was to position Wavely as the go-to financial app for young professionals looking to manage their finances with confidence.

THE CHALLENGE

Standing Out in a Crowded Market

Wavely had a solid product with powerful features, but their brand lacked personality and emotional connection. Their existing interface was functional but uninspiring, leading to low engagement and high drop-off rates.

  • Generic Brand Identity

    Their existing brand failed to differentiate them from competitors or connect with their target audience.

  • Complex User Experience

    The app's interface was cluttered and difficult to navigate, causing user frustration.

  • Low Conversion Rates

    Despite good traffic, sign-up and retention rates were below industry standards.

THE RESULTS

Measurable Impact

The rebrand and UX overhaul delivered impressive results across all key metrics, exceeding client expectations and industry benchmarks.

120% Increase in Signups

The streamlined onboarding process and compelling brand messaging significantly boosted conversion rates.

4.8 App Store Rating

User satisfaction soared, with the app rating improving from 3.6 to 4.8 stars.

35% Increase in Retention

The improved user experience led to higher engagement and significantly better retention rates.

Industry Recognition

The project won two design awards and was featured in several industry publications.
